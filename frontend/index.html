TruthLens

TruthLens is a transparent, rule-based misinformation verification tool designed to help users evaluate online content credibility before sharing.

How It Works

Source Evaluation
Checks the credibility of the news publisher
Language Analysis
Detects sensational or misleading wording
Rule-Based Scoring
Generates a transparent credibility score

Real-World Impact

Reduces Panic
Prevents viral spread of unverified emergency news
Encourages Critical Thinking
Assists users without enforcing censorship
Supports Journalists & Citizens
Acts as a decision-support verification layer
